EffortIQ Account & Data Deletion Request

EffortIQ Time Tracker is an internal enterprise application used by company employees.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your EffortIQ account and associated data, please send an email to:

Email: vijay@swaas.net

Subject: EffortIQ Account Deletion Request

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Login credentials
  • Timesheet records
  • Project activity data

Data Retention Timeline

All account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving the request.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team.
